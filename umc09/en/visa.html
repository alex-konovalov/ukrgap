Visa Information

The citizens of Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Canada, Cyprus, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Japan, Korea, Latvia, Liechtenstein, Lithuania, Luxemburg, Malta, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, UK, USA may enter Ukraine with valid passports of their respective countries for travel abroad for a period of up to 90 days (It would make sense to have an invitation letter).

To enter Ukraine, no visa is required for persons who hold an ordinary travel passports of Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Georgia, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyz Stan, Moldova, Russia, Tajikistan or Uzbekistan (It would make sense to have an invitation letter).

For citizens of  Argentina, Croatia, Mexico, Turkey faxed invitation from the Organizing Committee would be sufficient to apply to the Ukrainian consulates in their countries in order to get Ukrainian visa. Please request such invitation only when you are reasonably certain that you are coming to the congress.

Citizens of Brazil, Serbia, Republic of Montenegro, Venezuela need an original invitation from the Organizing Committee and to apply to the Ukrainian consulates in order to get Ukrainian visa. Please request such invitation only when you are reasonably certain that you are coming to the congress.

Citizens of Australia need a faxed invitation from the Institute of Mathematics (signed by the Director and with the official Institute seal) and to apply to the Ukrainian consulates in Australia in order to get Ukrainian visa. Please request such invitation when you are reasonably certain that you are coming to the congress.

Citizens of other countries (in particular, Algeria, P.R. China, Egypt, India, Iran, Jordan, Pakistan, Republic of South Africa, Saudi Arabia, Syria, Vietnam) need an original special invitation from the Organizing Committee and to apply to the Ukrainian consulate in their respective countries in order to get Ukrainian visa. To arrange such special invitation, we need the following additional information:

            1. Citizenship.
            2. Full name as in the passport.
            3. Date and place of birth.
            4. Home address.
            5. Work place and address.
            6. Current position.
            7. Passport No.
            8. Passport expiry date (with day and month).
            9. Country and city where you plan to apply to the Ukrainian consulate for obtaining visa.
            10. Scanned copy (.pdf or .jpg) of the first page of your passport, as well as copies of pages with your latest two-three visas (if available).

As soon as the invitation is ready we will mail the originals of the invitations to you by regular mail.
Please request such invitation only when you are reasonably certain that you are coming to the congress.
Deadline for application for the special invitation is June 15, 2009.

WARNING: Visa cannot be obtained in the airport upon arrival.
